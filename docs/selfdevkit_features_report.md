## Data Format & Conversion

### [JSON Tools](https://selfdevkit.com/features/json-tools/)

Format, validate, and manipulate JSON data. Includes JSON beautifier, minifier, and validator.

#### Features

- **Paste, Sample, Copy, Clear**: Quickly paste your own JSON, use sample data, copy results, or clear the editor with a single click.
- **Dual-pane JSON Viewer**: View and edit raw JSON on the left, and see a formatted, interactive, collapsible tree view on the right.
- **Search & Filter**: Instantly search and filter through your JSON data to find exactly what you need, even in large files.
- **Indentation & Formatting Options**: Choose your preferred indentation (2 or 4 spaces), toggle quote keys, show array indices, collection count, sort keys, and trailing commas.
- **Font Size & Collapse Controls**: Adjust font size for readability, and collapse or expand all nodes for easy navigation of complex structures.
- **Validation & Error Highlighting**: Instantly validate your JSON and get clear error messages for invalid structures.
- **Offline & Private**: All processing happens locally in your browser. Your data never leaves your device.
